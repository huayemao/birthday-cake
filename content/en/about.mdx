This is an innovative online birthday celebration tool that allows you to share birthday joy with friends and family over the internet.

No matter where you are, with just a link, you can invite distant friends and family to blow out candles on a virtual birthday cake through their microphone, creating a warm and unforgettable remote celebration experience.

## Features

✦ Multiple exquisite cake templates to choose from

✦ Support custom candle count and digit shapes

✦ Realistic flame animation effects

✦ Microphone-sensing blow candle feature

✦ Personalized messages and signatures

✦ One-click share link to friends
